About Me

After spending years in the New York City restuarant industry, I hit the road and traveled the world for a year and half before finally settling down in Spain.


For the last 6 years, I have called Madrid home, working as an English and Technology teacher in a Bilingual High school.


My passion for language and problem solving has led me to a career in software development. I love the concept of giving meaning to language; transforming code to beautiful and interactive web pages. Throughout my time as an educator, as well as traveling, I have continuously worked and collaborated with others to learn, create, and succeed.


When not teaching or learning code, I enjoy:


  • traveling and exploring new cultures
  • cooking and eating vegan cuisine
  • cycling through the city or along the water
